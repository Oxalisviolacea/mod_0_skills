Class: Pasta

Attributes:
1. noodle_type (string)
2. dairy_free (boolean)
3. cups_of_noodles (integer)
4. ingredients (array)
5. l1_cook_time (integer) - l1, left 1 refers to the position at the table

Methods:
1. lowercase_type (changes all the letters to lowercase)
2. dairy_free (checks array for "cheese" and "milk")
3. calculate_noodle_calories (multiplies cups_of_noodles by 352)
4. add_basil (adds "basil" to ingredients)
5. add_noodle_ingredient (adds noodle_type to list of ingredients)
6. add_cook_time: (adds the dish's cook time to an array with cook times for the table)
